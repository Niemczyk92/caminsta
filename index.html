MSP & Security

Proactive Security That Never Sleeps

Your business runs 24/7 — so does your attack surface. Caminsta's Managed Security Services combine continuous monitoring, enterprise-grade tooling, and human expertise to keep your organization protected, compliant, and operational. We don't wait for incidents. We prevent them.

Core Service Offerings
  • Managed Detection & Response (MDR) — Real-time threat detection across endpoints, network, and cloud with a guaranteed 15-minute response window. Our SOC analysts triage every alert — no alert fatigue, no missed threats, ever.
  • Endpoint Protection Platform (EPP/EDR) — Next-gen AV, behavioral analysis, and rollback capabilities on every device. CrowdStrike, SentinelOne, or Microsoft Defender Endpoint — we deploy and manage whichever fits your stack and budget.
  • Zero-Trust Network Architecture — Identity-based access control, micro-segmentation, and ZTNA policies that assume breach and limit blast radius. Built on proven frameworks including NIST SP 800-207.
  • Email Security & Anti-Phishing — Advanced threat protection for Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace. Includes link sandboxing, attachment detonation, DMARC/DKIM hardening, and quarterly phishing simulations with staff training.
  • Vulnerability Management — Continuous scanning, risk-ranked vulnerability reports, and coordinated patching across servers, workstations, and network devices. Nothing slips through untracked.
  • Compliance & Audit Readiness — Controls mapped to SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, and NIST CSF. We prepare you for audits with evidence collection, gap remediation, and auditor liaison support.
