Who We Are & What We Do

A Passion for
Craftsmanship
& Quality

Baltic Design Custom Marble and Granite is a leading stone design and fabrication company proudly serving South Florida. With a passion for craftsmanship and a commitment to quality, we specialize in creating custom marble and granite solutions tailored to enhance residential, commercial, and retail spaces.

From countertops and flooring to accent walls and decorative home features, our team works closely with clients to bring their unique vision to life. We source only the finest natural stone materials to ensure each project combines beauty with durability, offering timeless elegance that stands the test of time.

Our expert artisans take pride in delivering precision and attention to detail at every stage of the process, from design consultation to installation. As a certified general contractor, we adhere to the highest industry standards, ensuring reliability, professionalism, and lasting value with every project.

At Baltic Design, we believe in personalized service and a collaborative approach. We partner with homeowners, builders, and designers to create custom stone solutions that reflect each client's style and needs. Whether you're updating your kitchen, installing luxurious stone floors, or adding a striking accent wall, our goal is to exceed expectations with impeccable craftsmanship and competitive pricing.

Based in South Florida, Baltic Design Custom Marble and Granite combines local expertise with world-class materials to deliver stunning results. We are passionate about transforming spaces and helping our clients experience the elegance and sophistication of marble and granite.
